Privacy Policy

Dos is designed to keep your personal information on your device. This policy describes how the app handles the data you create while managing tasks and lists.

Information we collect

  • Task & list content. Titles, optional details, emojis, and due dates are saved locally in Core Data. They are never transmitted to Instabug or external services.
  • Speech dictation. When you hold the microphone button, iOS speech recognition converts your voice into text. The transcription is processed by Apple in accordance with your system settings and returned only to the app for insertion into the task field. Dos does not store audio or transcripts beyond your saved tasks.
  • App diagnostics. Dos does not include analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

How your information is used

Task and list data is used solely to display, sort, and sync content inside the app experience—lists, task detail, calendar view, and the celebratory completion screen. There is no server-side processing or aggregation.

Data storage & retention

All data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Removing a list permanently removes its tasks. Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device.

Third parties

Dos does not share information with third parties. The only external service touched is Apple’s speech recognition framework, which follows Apple’s privacy commitments.

Security

Because Dos relies on Apple’s hardened storage technologies, your content benefits from the same device-level encryption and biometric protections that secure the rest of iOS.

Your choices

  • Delete any list or task at any time using in-app controls to remove data immediately from local storage.
  • Disable speech recognition in iOS settings if you prefer not to use dictation.

Children’s privacy

Dos is intended for individuals 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
